Linear Programming Model Formulation

We formulate bandwidth allocation as a constrained Linear Program. The objective is to maximize the utility of allocated bandwidths xi weighted by traffic priorities ci, subject to total link capacity B and Class-of-Service QoS constraints:

Maximize   ∑ ci · xi
Subject to:
∑ xi ≤ B   (Total Link Capacity)
xi ≥ mi   (Guaranteed Minimum Bandwidth)
xi ≤ Mi   (Maximum Bandwidth Cap)

Convexity & Optimality: The objective function is linear (affine), and all constraints define a closed convex polytope. This LP is structurally convex, guaranteeing that the solution found by the CVXOPT interior-point solver is the globally optimal primal-dual pair.
